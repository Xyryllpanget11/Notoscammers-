How Scammers Operate

Scammers use various tactics, such as phishing emails, fake websites, and social engineering, to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information.

Prevention Tips

  • Be cautious with unsolicited emails or messages.
  • Verify the legitimacy of websites before providing personal information.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
  • Beware of investment opportunities that promise high returns with low risk.
  • Never give out your personal information to someone you don't trust.

How to Protect Yourself

Stay vigilant and follow these steps:

  1. Regularly update your software and antivirus programs.
  2. Educate yourself on common scam tactics.
  3. Keep personal information private and secure.
  4. Be careful about what information you share on social media.
  5. If you think you may have been the victim of a scam, contact your bank or credit card company immediately.
